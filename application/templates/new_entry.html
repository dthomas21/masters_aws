Important Reminders
Entrants are allowed to submit up to three teams. Team Name(s) must be independent of each other.

There is an entry fee of $30 per team. In terms of payout, there will be first, second, and third place
winners who will take home 70%, 20%, and 10% of the pot, respectively. Winners will be determined by the
highest aggregate tournament dollars.

Tie Breaker
Please indicate the total number of strokes under par you think your team will finish with at the conclusion
of the tournament. In the case of a tie between entrants, this number will determine the winner of the pool.
